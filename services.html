Our Services

  • Technique Tuneups

    Our levels for our competitive swimmers are divided into 3 levels, Bronze, Silver, and Gold. Our instructors will decide what level you are when you join. All our instructors are constantly updated on the latest swimming technique in the swim industry.

  • Swim Lessons

    Determination Aquatics’ most popular program are swim lessons. Before we begin teaching your swimmer, we will perform an evaluation to see what they need to learn and which lessons will be performed. Just like our technique tuneups, swim lessons will also have 3 levels and they will be divided by advancement.

  • Lifeguard Services

    Another cool thing that we offer is renting a lifeguard out. Some of our staff are lifeguard certified and we are available for events like pool parties and private gatherings. Please reach to lifeguard@swimdna.org for inquiries on renting lifeguards and we will be more than happy to assist you and your group!

  • Meet Hosting

    At SwimDNA, not only do we offer lessons and lifeguarding, but our staff is also very skilled in running meets. We organize the meet, do meet entries, and run the meet all for you. All you have to do is provide a facility, touchpads, plungers, stopwatches, and a timing system (ex. CTS System 6). For an additional fee, we offer for you to rent out a Colarado Gen7 and/or Dolphin wireless stopwatches. Email us at entrychair@swimdna.org for booking, quotes, and/or more info.
